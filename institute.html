The Institute

What is the conflict you may ask? Well, it has to do with robots–human-like robots. You see, there is a boogeyman in the Commonwealth: the Institute. The Institute acts as the scientific, futuristic faction that wants to blend humans with technology. They created robots–which are called “synths” in the game–which can hardly be distinguished from a human, unless you pick apart their insides. It is at first rumored, then confirmed, that this faction captures humans in the night and replaces them with identical synths. There is some dark lore to this faction, and I suggest playing the game to discover what they might be hiding.
