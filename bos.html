The Brotherhood Of Steel (BOS)

Now as you can probably guess, there are people who agree and people who disagree on whether the Institute belongs in the Commonwealth. Immediately upon meeting the Brotherhood of Steel, you will know that they do not tolerate the Institute and their practices.

The dynamic of the Brotherhood of Steel (BOS) is more military-like, with ranks and all. They seek to acquire pre-war technologies and fight for the good of the Commonwealth. Most notably, they are identified by their power armor.
